Privacy Policy

VIRL is built on the principle that your real-world interactions belong to you. Our mission is to help you look up from your screen, not to track every breath you take. We only collect the data necessary to gamify your experiences and reward your growth.

1. The Data We Value

We collect your name and email to manage your account. We use location data exclusively to verify mission completion, because "touching grass" doesn't count if you're doing it in a simulator. This data is used only for game mechanics and is never sold to third parties.

2. Ownership

You own your stats. If you want to delete your mission history or your account entirely, you can do it with a single tap. We don't believe in "sticky" platforms; we succeed when you put the phone down.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption to keep your logins and results safe. Your missions are for your character progression, not for public surveillance.
