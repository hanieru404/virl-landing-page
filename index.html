Common

What even is VIRL?

VIRL turns your real life into a game. Complete daily missions, earn XP, and actually go outside and do things instead of just scrolling.

How do the missions work?

Every day you get a set of real-world challenges to complete, like touching grass, starting a conversation with a stranger, or exploring somewhere new. Finish them, get rewarded.

Is this just another productivity app?

Not even close. This is not about building habits or being a better person. It is about making life feel less boring and more worth showing up for.

Who is this for?

Anyone who feels like they spend too much time on their phone and not enough time actually living. Mostly people in their late teens and twenties.

What do I actually win?

XP, badges, and in-app rewards that you unlock as you level up. Your character grows the more you show up in real life.

Do I need friends to play?

Nope. You can go solo or link up with people. Either way works.

Is it free?

The core experience is free. There will be optional stuff you can unlock later, but you will not need to spend anything to enjoy it.

When does it launch?

We are still building. Join the waitlist and you will be first to know, plus you get early access perks.

What makes this different from other apps?

Most apps want to keep you on the screen. VIRL literally rewards you for putting it down and going out.

How do I get in early?

Drop your name and email on the waitlist. Simple as that.
